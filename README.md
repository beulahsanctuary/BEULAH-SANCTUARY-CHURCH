# BEULAH-SANCTUARY-CHURCH
📖 About Us

Beulah Sanctuary is a Christ-centered church located in Mshomoroni, Mombasa, committed to spreading the Gospel of Jesus Christ and serving the local community with love, compassion, and integrity.

Our name “Beulah” means “married” (Isaiah 62:4), symbolizing restoration, joy, and a deep covenant relationship with God. At Beulah Sanctuary, we believe in creating a welcoming spiritual home where people encounter God, grow in faith, and build meaningful relationships.

🎯 Our Mission

To transform lives through:

The preaching and teaching of the Word of God

Prayer and worship

Discipleship and spiritual growth

Community outreach and service

🙏 Our Core Values

Faith – Trusting God in all things

Love – Serving others with Christ-like compassion

Integrity – Living upright and accountable lives

Unity – Growing together as one body in Christ

Excellence – Giving God our very best

Beulah Sanctuary
